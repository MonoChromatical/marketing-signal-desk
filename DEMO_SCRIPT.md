# Loom Walkthrough Script

Target length: 3 to 5 minutes.

## 1. Opening

Signal Desk is an AI-first media buying command center. It takes messy ad platform exports and turns them into a ranked action queue: what to pause, what to scale, what to investigate, and what test to launch next.

## 2. Data Intake

Click **Run Full Demo**. The demo loads a realistic sample account with Meta, Google, TikTok, and Taboola-style campaign rows.

## 3. Account Triage

Point out spend, leads, qualified CPL, and budget at risk. The important detail is that the app optimizes for qualified leads, not just cheap form fills.

## 4. Action Queue

Open the top recommendation. Explain that the backend ranks actions by estimated impact and attaches evidence: spend, qualified CPL, CTR, conversion rate, and confidence.

## 5. AI Operator

Show the daily action memo and ad copy ideas. Explain that OpenAI can upgrade the output, but deterministic agent fallback keeps the team operating when provider quota or availability is unavailable.

## 6. Experiment Plan

Show the generated experiment plan. Explain that the output is ready for a media buyer: hypothesis, launch plan, success rule, kill rule, and metrics to watch.

## 7. Code/Architecture

Mention FastAPI, Pydantic schemas, a separated analysis engine, optional AI layer, and tool-shaped backend functions that can become MCP tools.

## 8. Next Steps

Live ad platform connectors, CRM lead quality ingestion, Slack daily delivery, landing page crawler, and approval workflows for budget changes.
